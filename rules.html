📜 Official Rules – Baca Summer Challenge 2026

Overview

Duration: 9 weeks

Start date: Monday, June 8, 2026

End date: Sunday, August 9, 2026 (makeups through Tuesday, August 11, 2026 ok)

Weekly Reading Targets

  • Adele: 50 minutes/week (about 10 minutes/weekday)
  • Reed: 100 minutes/week (about 20 minutes/weekday)
  • Soren: 150 minutes/week (about 30 minutes/weekday)
Adele: Being read to by Soren, Reed, Mom, Dad or Ana Rita counts! Note: Bedtime reading or reading to Nana does not count.

How You Earn Money

For every extra daily session's worth of reading above your weekly target, you earn $5. That means:

  • Adele: every extra 10 minutes above her weekly target earns $5
  • Reed: every extra 20 minutes above his weekly target earns $5
  • Soren: every extra 30 minutes above his weekly target earns $5

Only whole sessions count — partial sessions don't earn money.

Examples:
Adele reads 80 min (target 50) → 30 extra → 3 sessions → $15!
Reed reads 140 min (target 100) → 40 extra → 2 sessions → $10!
Soren reads 210 min (target 150) → 60 extra → 2 sessions → $10!

The first $100 earned goes toward your Only Child Yes Day. Anything above $100 goes into your Greenlight investing account.

⭐ Family Treat Goal

Every time siblings read together, the whole family earns stars toward a Family Treat!

  • Soren reads to Adele (10+ minutes) = 1 star
  • Reed reads to Adele (10+ minutes) = 1 star
  • Adele reads to Reed (5+ minutes) = 1 star
  • Adele reads to Soren (5+ minutes) = 1 star

Maximum 4 stars per day. Every star = $1 toward the Family Treat Goal.

The Make-Up Rule

If you don't hit your target one week, you can make it up by reading extra in a later week. Make-up minutes count toward erasing your shortfall first, before they start earning you money. No make-ups after the first day of school.

Example: Reed's target is 100 min. Week 1 he reads 70 min (30 short). Week 2 he reads 160 min. The first 30 extra erase the shortfall. The remaining 30 extra (1 full 20-min session) earn $5.

🏆 The Big Prize: Only Child Yes Day

If you hit your weekly target all 9 weeks (including make-ups), you earn an Only Child Yes Day in the fall!

  • You pick one parent to spend the whole day with — just the two of you
  • You plan the day together — outing, time at home, or a mix
  • It happens in the fall!

✉️ Bonus Writing Challenge

Any time during the summer where you write a letter to anyone (cousin, grandparent, etc.) and mail it, you get $2 towards your summer earnings. Unlimited reward!

The Fine Print

Mom and Dad will be the final adjudicators of any rule interpretations or disputes. Mom and Dad reserve the right to modify the rules for fairness throughout the summer, proactively or retroactively.
